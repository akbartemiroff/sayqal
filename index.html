About Restaurant Sayqal

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Restaurant Sayqal has been serving authentic Uzbek cuisine to food lovers. Our restaurant combines traditional recipes with modern culinary techniques to create unique dining experiences.

We take pride in using only the freshest ingredients and preparing our dishes with care. Our commitment to quality has made us a favorite destination for those seeking authentic Uzbek flavors.
